## 🧑‍🏫 Jobs & Professions

Firefighter, chef, pilot, game developer, scientist, explorer, zookeeper, teacher, astronaut, police officer, doctor, artist, etc.

### What they can write about:

<ul>
  <li>What the job is about</li>
  <li>Daily tasks and responsibilities</li>
  <li>Tools or equipment used</li>
  <li>Skills needed to do the job well</li>
  <li>Why the job is important</li>
  <li>Where people in this job usually work</li>
  <li>Uniforms or dress code (if any)</li>
  <li>Famous people with that job (if applicable)</li>
  <li>Why they would or wouldn't want to do this job</li>
</ul>
